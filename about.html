Catherine Barranco

Republican Candidate · South Dakota State Auditor · 2026

A Leader Ready to Serve

Catherine Barranco is a South Dakotan committed to conservative principles, fiscal responsibility, and accountable government. With a career spanning corporate finance, federal oversight, and Republican Party leadership, she brings precisely the expertise South Dakota needs in its next State Auditor.

Over the past year, Catherine traveled across South Dakota alongside her husband David — attending Lincoln Day dinners and candidate forums statewide — as his closest adviser and trusted partner in public service. When David received a Stage IV cancer diagnosis and was no longer able to continue, he asked Catherine to carry forward the campaign they had built together.

"The people of South Dakota deserve a state auditor who will fight for them with unwavering integrity — a proven leader with the education and experience necessary to succeed. I made David a solemn vow to honor his wishes and continue his legacy."

— Catherine Barranco

She stood beside him through every challenge and every success. Now she is ready to lead — bringing her own considerable credentials to the office and the mission they share: ensuring South Dakota's taxpayer-funded checkbook is balanced with transparency, integrity, and fiscal prudence.

"If you think I'm a hard worker now," she says, "just wait — you ain't seen nothing yet."
